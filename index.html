Our Services

Sales

Selling your home is a serious business. It is imperative that you appoint the right agent to promote your property, protect your interest and achieve the best possible result. You can rest assured that Vousden’s will take care of your move from start to finish with an enthusiastic, diligent and professional approach and are totally committed to delivering the results you expect.

Valuations

Valuing a property correctly is crucial to the successful sale of your home. Pitch the price too high and your property may compare unfavourably with others of the same price and you risk losing buyers; too low and buyers may question what is wrong with it. We have very quickly established a strong presence in the area and are in a position to utilise our extensive local knowledge to provide you with an accurate assessment of your home backed up by professional marketing advice.

Mortgages

We work with an extremely reputable independent expert mortgage broker, who are not restricted to using a specific lender or group of lenders and can easily compare the cheapest rates offered by all banks. The advantage of this is that it cuts out the hassle of speaking directly with a wide range of banks in order to find out which lender is cheapest in any given scenario. Some lenders, particularly for specialist solutions, only offer specific products through mortgage brokers.
